Cities to Explore

Kuala Lumpur

Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia, its known for its shopping and dining, with options ranging from large malls and contemporary restaurants to shophouses and food stalls.

Kuching

Kuching is a bustling, diverse city of old colonial buildings and modern towers. Along its Sarawak River waterfront are food vendors, shophouses selling handicrafts and an esplanade with views of 19th-century landmarks.

Langkawi

Langkawi, the Jewel of Kedah, is a district and an archipelago of 99 islands in the Andaman Sea some 30 km off the mainland coast of northwestern Malaysia.
